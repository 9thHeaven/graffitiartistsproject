During the January 25, 2011 Egyptian revolution, female Egyptian street artists used public spaces to bring awareness to the endless social injustices that Egyptian women face on a daily basis.

Graffiti is dangerous work and these street artists demonstrate the perseverance,grace, and creativity needed to create on dialogue on gender violence, economic advancement, and political rights.

Their works are detailed, huge, and sometimes controversial. These women use graffiti and street art to create an identity for themselves that reflects their personalities, ideas, and strengths.
